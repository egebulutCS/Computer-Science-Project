Cognitive Biometric Graphical Authentication of Users

This experiment will require a commitment of at least 3 sessions from each participant.

It is recommended to have at least 1 hour between sessions.

The pattern is supposed to be a personal password.

Data that is submitted by the participants will be kept anonymously and protected by password. It will be used in the analysis of the project's aim.

The data collected will be solely based on the interactions of participants with the grids.

Participants may withdraw at any moment.

Participants cannot be under the age of 18.
